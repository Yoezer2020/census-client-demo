<h2>Way Forward</h2>

<p>
  The DCRC in 2003 initiated the computerisation of citizen's records and
  simultaneously started the development of Bhutan Civil Registration System
  (BCRS) with the automated citizenship identity card personalisation and
  issuance system. The BCRS has all the necessary security features in the place
  to address earlier concerns. With this system, the DCRC centrally managed the
  entire civil registration and monitor the issuance of citizenship identity
  cards.
</p>

<h3>Planned Initiatives</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Initiate appointment system for identified services.</li>
  <li>
    Initiate levying of fees for identified DCRC services and revision of fees
    for CID / Special Resident Card.
  </li>
  <li>Online access of Household Information / Family Register to Gewogs.</li>
  <li>Submit proposal on HR requirement to the Ministry.</li>

</ul>
