## Body HTML (paste into the CMS Body field)

```html
<h2>Vision</h2>

<blockquote>
  To become an efficient and effective organization to maintain demographic
  records and deliver the highest standard of civil registration and census
  related services.
</blockquote>

<h2>Mission</h2>

<p>In order to fulfill the above vision, the Department is mandated to:</p>

<ul>
  <li>
    Develop and maintain accurate and complete information on population and
    demography.
  </li>
  <li>Develop comprehensive Civil Registration and Vital Statistics System.</li>
  <li>
    Enhance the security and authentication features of Citizenship Card,
    Special Resident Card and other breeder documents.
  </li>
  <li>
    Ensure professional service delivery through optimal use of Information
    Communication and Technology.
  </li>
  <li>
    Provide timely, relevant and reliable demographic statistics to competent
    authority for evidence based policy and decision making.
  </li>
</ul>

<h2>Core Values</h2>

<p>
  Guided by principles to promote professionalism and client satisfactory
  services.
</p>

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Value</th>
      <th>Description</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Integrity</strong></td>
      <td>
        Uphold the interest of the Department and public above personal interest
        while performing duty.
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Professionalism</strong></td>
      <td>
        Recruit and deploy competent employees to provide professional services
        at all times.
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Adaptability</strong></td>
      <td>
        Respond to change with positive attitude and willingness to learn in
        changing work environments.
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Team Work</strong></td>
      <td>
        Build and promote cohesive and affirmative inter-personal relationships
        among the employees.
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Client Satisfactory Services</strong></td>
      <td>
        Create enabling environment for efficient delivery, and respond to the
        needs of the clients.
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Timeliness</strong></td>
      <td>Display punctuality and provide timely services to all citizens.</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
```

---

## Notes

- The `<blockquote>` tag is styled with a left border in the Client Portal — ideal for the Vision statement.
- The Core Values table replaces the card grid in the static page. Update cell text here to change what appears on the portal.
- To add a new value, insert a new `<tr>` row in the `<tbody>`.
